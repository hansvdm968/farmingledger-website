Security

Reasonable safeguards for farm records.

Security measures help protect Farming Ledger, but no website, app, database, server, or transmission method is completely free from risk.

Our safeguards

Farming Ledger implements reasonable technical and organisational measures intended to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the website, mobile application, user accounts, and stored information. These measures may include controlled access, authentication requirements, hosting safeguards, monitoring, backups, and service-provider protections.

Your responsibility

You are responsible for keeping login credentials confidential, limiting access to your devices, and ensuring that information submitted by you is accurate, lawful, and appropriately protected on your side. You use the website, app, and services at your own risk.

Incidents

If we detect or reasonably suspect a security incident, we may investigate, suspend access, reset credentials, remove content, or take any other measure we consider necessary to protect the services, users, or third parties.

Limitations

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Farming Ledger disclaims liability for security breaches, interception, outages, corruption, delays, unauthorised access, or losses arising from events outside our reasonable control, third-party infrastructure, user negligence, or misuse of the services.

Contact

If you find a security issue, email info@vdmfarming.co.za with the affected page, device, browser, and steps to reproduce the issue.
