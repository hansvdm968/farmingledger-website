Privacy policy

How Farming Ledger handles information.

This policy explains the practical privacy position for the website, downloads, and Farming Ledger app experience.

Information we collect

When you contact us, we may receive your email address and the message you send. When you use the app, the app may store account details and farm records such as finance, crops, poultry, harvests, inventory, fields, and related usage information needed to operate the service.

How we use information

We use information to provide the app, respond to support requests, manage access, improve reliability, process subscriptions, and keep the website and downloads working. We do not claim to run advertising tracking on this website.

Browser and device storage

The website and app may use local storage, browser cache, and service-worker caching so pages and app files load reliably. Some contact form entries may be saved on your own device by the browser until you clear site data.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. We may use trusted hosting, database, payment, or support providers where needed to run Farming Ledger. We keep information for as long as it is needed for service, legal, billing, security, or support purposes.

Questions

For privacy questions or access requests, contact hello@FarmingLedger.co.za.
